As part of the Design Process and Practice Module we were given an assignment to complete. We had to work on a random theme and create a poster design which would represent our theme, Our group did Mental Health.

Below is a collage of images detailing our work, including my final poster design on the left a photo of our wall on the bottom and a photo of us working as a team on the right.

For my final project I wanted to do something humourous yet serious regarding the subject, so I decided to create this poster with using a bee related pun with the message "Don't fall BEE-hind on your Mental Health"
